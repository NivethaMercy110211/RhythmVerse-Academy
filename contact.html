FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have got answers. Here are the most common questions we receive about RhythmVerse Academy.

What is the minimum age to enroll at RhythmVerse Academy?
We welcome students from age 5 onwards. Our Kids Special Program is designed for children aged 5–14, and our regular batches cater to teenagers and adults of all ages. There is no upper age limit — dance is for everyone!
Do I need prior dance experience to join?
Absolutely not! Our Beginner Foundation batches are designed specifically for absolute beginners. We assess every new student and place them in the most suitable batch. Your first class is free, so you can experience the learning environment before committing.
How much does enrollment cost per month?
Monthly fees vary by dance style and batch level, ranging from ₹2,000 to ₹4,500 per month. A registration fee of ₹500 is applicable for new students. Contact our admissions team for a detailed fee structure specific to your chosen program.
Can I switch dance styles after enrolling?
Yes! Students can request a style transfer or add a second style after completing at least one full month. Speak to our admissions team and they will help you make the switch smoothly without any disruption to your learning.
Are costumes and props included in the fee?
Basic practice attire guidance is provided free of cost. Performance costumes for recitals and events are provided by the academy at a nominal additional cost. We work with trusted costume designers to ensure beautiful, affordable performance wear for all students.
Is there a trial class before full enrollment?
Yes! We offer a completely free, no-obligation 60-minute trial class in any of our available batches. Simply fill in the enrollment enquiry form above or call us to schedule your trial class. We encourage every new student to try before committing.
