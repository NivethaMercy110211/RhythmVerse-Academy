Our Dance Styles

Six distinct disciplines — each with its own language, history and energy. Find the style that speaks to your soul.

Classical Western Hip-Hop Salsa Contemporary Freestyle

Style 01

Classical Dance

Rooted in centuries-old traditions, classical dance is the foundation of all Indian performing arts. Our program covers Bharatanatyam, Kathak and Odissi — taught in authentic lineage style by Guru Priya Anand.

Students progress through systematic arangetram preparation, mudra training, abhinaya expression, and thalam (rhythmic) mastery.

Bharatanatyam Kathak Odissi Mudra Training

Style 02

Western Dance

A dynamic fusion of ballet technique with modern stage flair, our Western dance program covers Jazz, Contemporary Ballet, and Lyrical dance. Ideal for students seeking a versatile performance toolkit.

Classes focus on flexibility, extension, partnering and stage presence — building a strong technical base that can be applied across many styles.

Jazz Lyrical Contemporary Ballet Stage Performance
Enroll Now Book Trial

Style 03

Hip-Hop

Born on the streets of New York, hip-hop is the most expressive and dynamic of all urban dance forms. Our program covers Breaking, Popping, Locking, Krumping and New Style Hip-Hop under senior instructor Rajan Mehta.

Our batch structure takes you from foundational grooves to full-length choreographic routines ready for competition and stage.

Breaking Popping Locking New Style
Enroll Now Book Trial

Style 04

Salsa

Salsa is passion, connection and rhythm in pure form. Led by Latin dance champion Sofia Reyes, our Salsa program covers Cuban style, New York On2, Bachata and social dance etiquette — both solo and partner work.

Our special Couples batch makes salsa learning accessible and fun for partners at any level of dance experience.

Cuban Salsa Bachata Partner Work Couples Batch
Enroll Now Book Trial

Style 05

Contemporary

Contemporary dance defies boundaries. It blends elements of modern, jazz, ballet, and lyrical into a fluid, emotion-driven form of expression. Our instructors guide students through improvisation, floor work, and compositional elements.

Classes focus on body weight distribution, fluid transitions, and creative expression — helping you build a deeper emotional connection with the music.

Modern Technique Floor Work Improvisation Composition
Enroll Now Book Trial

Style 06

Freestyle

Freestyle is where all rules dissolve and your unique voice as a dancer takes over. This program is designed for students who have foundational training in any style and want to develop their personal choreographic signature.

Our sessions provide guided battle practice, musical interpretation, and choreographic tools to help you break free from patterns and dance with true freedom.

Fusion Choreography Battle Prep Personal Style Dev Musicality
